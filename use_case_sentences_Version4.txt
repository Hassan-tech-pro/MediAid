Use Case 1: Common Cold
The patient is experiencing continuous sneezing, chills, fatigue, cough, high fever, and headache.

Use Case 2: Diabetes
The patient presents with fatigue, weight loss, restlessness, lethargy, irregular sugar level, blurred and distorted vision, obesity, excessive hunger, increased appetite, and polyuria.

Use Case 3: Migraine
The patient is suffering from acidity, indigestion, headache, blurred and distorted vision, excessive hunger, stiff neck, depression, irritability, and visual disturbances.

Use Case 4: Chicken Pox
The patient complains of itching, skin rash, fatigue, lethargy, high fever, headache, loss of appetite, mild fever, swelled lymph nodes, and malaise.

Use Case 5: Hypertension
The patient reports headache, chest pain, dizziness, loss of balance, and lack of concentration.

Use Case 6: Fungal Infection
The patient has itching, skin rash, nodal skin eruptions, and dischromic patches.
